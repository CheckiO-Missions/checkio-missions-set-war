The battle is still continue and every army are losing the good warriors. Let's try to fix it and add new unit type - Healer.
Healer won't fight by himself (his attack = 0 so he can't deal the damage), but his role is also very important - every time the allied soldier hits the enemy, the Healer will heal the allie, who stands right before him by +2 health points with the heal() method. Consider that the health after healing is can't be greater than the maximum health of th unit. For example, if the Healer heals the Warrior with 49 health points, the Warrior will have 50 hp, because this is the maximum for him.
The basic parameters of the Healer:
health = 60
attack = 0
